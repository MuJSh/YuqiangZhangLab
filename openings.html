PhD and Master students

We recruit 1-3 PhD students and 1-2 Master students each year. If you are interested in working with us, please send us an email.
In the email, please
(1) state briefly why you are interested in us,
(2) attach a CV,
(3) attach your Master (if any) and Undergraduate transcripts,
(4) provide contact information of 1-2 references.

博士与硕士研究生

研究组每年招收1至3名博士研究生和1至2名硕士研究生。如果您有志加入我们，请邮件联系。
在邮件里，请您
（1）简要说明您为何对我们感兴趣；
（2）附上个人简历；
（3）附上硕士（如果有）及本科阶段成绩单;
（4）提供1至2名推荐人的联系方式。
